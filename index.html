Before you join

Clear answers for testers.

This page is for product testing, not purchasing.

Do testers have to pay?

No. Tester access is provided so you can use the early build and give useful feedback.

Does signing up guarantee access?

No. Invitations are sent in manageable waves so feedback can be reviewed properly.

What feedback is useful?

Specific examples are best: what you clicked, what you expected, what happened and any screenshots that show the problem.

How long will testing take?

Most testers should use the app normally for several days, then answer a short set of feedback questions.

Is TimeTrack designed for employee monitoring?

No. TimeTrack is designed for personal awareness and self-tracking on your own Windows PC.

Can I leave the testing list?

Yes. You can ignore an invitation or ask to be removed from future testing emails at any time.
